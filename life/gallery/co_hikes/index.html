colorado hikes

Mt. Tabeguache & Mt. Shavano (Spetember 27th, 2014)

I went down to the southern Sawatch to soke up those fall colors. I had perfect weather and the aspens were lovely.

Kit Carson & Challenger Point (August 30th, 2014)

Jacob, Chris and I planned a Labor Day backpacking trip down in the Sangre de Cristo Mountains. It was incredible. I got after the North Ridge.

Mount of the Holy Cross (August 24th, 2014)

My roommates had climbed Mount of the Holy Cross earlier in the summer, and I didn't want to be left behind! I spent the night in the Notch Mountain shelter at 13,200 ft. I wasn't alone though, a pika nibbled on my hat while I was snoozing.

Long's Peak (August 16th, 2014)

After visiting a friend in NH (and climbing Mt. Washington!), I came back to CO visited Long's Peak. My friend Jake flew out from VA to join in the fun. The group was Jake, Jacob, Chris, and Alex. We climbed the Key Hole Route and rappelled down the Cables Route. It was a great time.

Democrat, Cameron, Lincoln, & Bross (August 3rd, 2014)

I took a week off to go on a family vacation on the East coast, but I was back for more the following weekend. My friend Jacob joined me for the DeCaLiBron.

Greys Peak and Torreys Peak (July 19th, 2014)

I had the 14er bug at this point. I headed up to the mountains on Friday night with the plan of hiking Greys and Torreys by myself the following morning. And I did just that.

Quandary Peak (July 12th, 2014)

Convinced some friends to go on a moonlight hike up Quandary and watch the sunrise from the top. Dante made friends.

Wilson Peak (July 4th, 2014)

I took a long hiatus from the mountains but finally got back with an Independence Day hike on Wilson Peak. It was my first taste of the San Juans. Telluride was beautiful!

Mt. Evans and Mt. Bierstadt (Aug. 17th, 2013)

These were my very first fourteeners. My roommates dragged me up them exactly 2 weeks after I moved to the beautiful state of Colorado!
